I live in Waterford, Michigan, and work as a software engineer at Rocket Mortgage.

I build open-source cards for Home Assistant, including Chronicle Card, a universal timeline card in the HACS default store, Air Quality Card, for indoor air-quality monitoring with WHO-based health thresholds, and Garage Card, a top-down visual garage.

I also run kdentee LLC, a small studio for freelance web work, where I designed and still maintain the website for Lincoln Township, Michigan.

Most of the software I use at home is my own. My homelab runs Crate, an inventory system for the physical world, and Marquee, a household tracker for TV, movies, and anime.

I enjoy building small, polished tools that solve real problems.

You can find me on GitHub, LinkedIn, read my blog, or reach me via email.
